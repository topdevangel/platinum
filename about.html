About

Cathedra Public School is a wonderful and enormous community that includes many people indirectly but directly students, parents and staff, who work with the purpose of creating the best academically excellent, culturally supportive and inclusive learning environment. We strive to improve, be great, and influence society in a positive way!

We are champions of knowledge of our urban environment, of the environment and of our common future.

Invento achievers are goal-oriented and critical thinkers. We are community leaders who are dedicated to achieving the highest level of integrity and academic success.

The administration and staff work in harmony to provide a challenging learning environment without neglecting support where it is needed so that everyone has the opportunity to achieve personal success and reach their full potential. Each member participates in lifelong learning and improvement cycles that include setting high expectations, setting goals, planning, action, and reflection.
